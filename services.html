Srisailam Tourism

The best time to visit Srisailam is from September to March while the peak season is September to December & February to May. Usually it takes 1-2 days to visit major places in Srisailam October to February offers the best climate for tourist activities and pilgrimage.Srisailam Sanctuary, the Phaladhara Panchadhara, the Srisailam Dam View Point etc. The temperature remains pleasant between 15°C and 32°C, making it an excellent time to go sightseeing.
